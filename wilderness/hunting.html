Hunting

Hunting takes 4 turns during early morning or late evening hours. Hunting in off hours gives a penalty. Every hunter after the first gives a penalty (but each hunter may roll and more hunters may bring back more game). Missiles must cause more than 1d4 damage to bring down large game.
